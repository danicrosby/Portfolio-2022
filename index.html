About

Hello, I recently completed the front-end engineering bootcamp at Nashville Software School, where I studied Javascript & React. I'm currently enrolled in the UI & UX Digital Design course, where I just wrapped up teaching a series of HTML & CSS classes. I will complete my UI & UX certificate in early May 2022.

Background

I have a background in a variety of areas, including home organization, management, and as a small business owner. I was also a welder for three years before attending dental school in Saint Louis, Missouri.

I find joy in finding ways to make whatever I'm working on smooth and efficient. For example, I traveled internationally to test and organize floor layouts for a multi-million dollar, state-of-the-art robotic dashboard assembly line for a major player in the automotive industry. I provided feedback on my findings on ways to create faster production. Those suggestions were applied and used in several plants within the U.S. & Mexico.

Design Style

I'm a minimalist drawn to clean lines and generous white space. I have a great deal of passion for simplifying application interfaces. With simple designs, I to help people who struggle with technology navigate the web and apps easier, specifically older folks.
